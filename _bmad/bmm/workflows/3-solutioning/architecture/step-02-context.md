## Project Context Submission

**Main Goals:**
- Extend the lifecycle of smart home devices by making repair and maintenance more accessible, affordable, and sustainable.
- Reduce e-waste and support a sustainable digital future.

**Key Features & Requirements:**
- AI-Powered Diagnostics: Real-time fault detection, automated troubleshooting using device performance, error codes, and user inputs.
- Predictive Maintenance: Proactive alerts forecasting potential device issues and suggesting preventive measures.
- Guided Repair Tutorials: Step-by-step guides with videos, visuals, and optional voice-assistant support for hands-free use.
- Cross-Brand Compatibility: Centralized dashboard to sync, monitor, and repair devices from any brand.
- Parts & Spare Parts Assistance: Information on required tools and spare parts, with direct purchase links to verified vendors.
- Technician Assistance: Find nearby certified technicians, book appointments, and access instant support.
- Community Forum: Peer-to-peer support for sharing experiences, tips, and troubleshooting advice.

**Critical Requirements/Constraints:**
- Mobile-first design.
- Scalable integration across diverse smart home ecosystems.
- Secure handling of user/device data.
- Reliable AI-driven analytics and natural language understanding.

**Stakeholders & Roles:**
- End Users: Homeowners and smart device users seeking repair and maintenance.
- Technicians: Certified professionals providing repair services and support.
- Vendors: Suppliers of tools and spare parts.
- AI Developers: Build diagnostic/predictive models, enable natural language understanding, ensure scalable integration.
- Community Members: Users sharing knowledge and support.

**Existing Systems/Technologies/Integrations:**
- Smart home devices from various brands and ecosystems.
- AI analytics engines for diagnostics and predictive maintenance.
- Natural language processing modules for user queries.
- Integration with vendor APIs for parts and technician services.
